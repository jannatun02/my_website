My Education

Discover my academic journey and milestones

Primary School

Institution: Sekolah Rendah Islam Hira'

Year 2011 - 2016

During my time in primary school, I was actively involved in co-curricular activities, specifically in TKRS (Tunas Kadet Remaja Sekolah). I dedicated myself to this program and achieved the rank of Corporal, which reflected my commitment and discipline. As a Corporal, I took on a leadership role, leading the house team during parade drills. Additionally, I was passionate about archery and regularly participated in both training and competitions, further developing my skills in focus and precision. Moreover in education, I got 3A for my UPSR.

High School

Institution: Sekolah Menengah Islam Hira'

Year 2017 - 2021

During my high school years, I continued to actively engage in co-curricular activities, particularly in KRS (Kadet Remaja Sekolah), where I further developed my leadership and organizational skills. Academically, I achieved strong results, earning 4A in PT3 and 3A in SPM, reflecting my commitment to balancing both my studies and extracurricular activities. These achievements not only strengthened my academic foundation but also taught me time management, responsibility, and the importance of hard work in all areas of life.

Diploma in Information Management

Institution: Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM)

Year 2022 - Present

Currently, I am pursuing a diploma in Information Management at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM), where I am gaining a deeper understanding of how to organize and manage knowledge in the digital age. This program has allowed me to explore various aspects of information systems, data management, and the importance of digital tools in efficiently handling vast amounts of information. Through this course, I am developing the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in the ever-evolving field of information management.
